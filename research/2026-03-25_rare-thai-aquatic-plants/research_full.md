## 5. Logistics & Legal

### CITES Status
- ❌ **Banned:** *Crinum thaianum* (Thai Onion Plant) — endangered, strictly prohibited
- ⚠️ **Restricted:** Orchids and pitcher plants (*Nepenthes*) require CITES Appendix II permits
- ✅ **Most aquarium plants:** NOT CITES-listed — require **Certificate of Unlisted Aquatic Species** from Thai Dept of Fisheries

### Documents Required
| Document | Issuer | Cost |
|----------|--------|------|
| Phytosanitary Certificate | Thai Dept of Agriculture | ฿100 official + ฿1,500–2,000 service fee |
| Certificate of Unlisted Aquatic Species | Thai Dept of Fisheries | ฿200–500 |

### Packaging
- **Tissue Culture (TC):** Sterile agar/gel. Zero soil. 99% customs clearance. Best option.
- **Bare Root:** 100% soil-free. Damp (not wet) paper towel. 4-mil poly bag + oxygen. Risky for long transit.

### Import Regulations (USA — Updated Aug 2025)
- $800 duty-free limit **removed**
- All shipments subject to **19% reciprocal tariff** + formal customs entry
- USDA PPQ 587 Import Permit: free, 24-hour approval
