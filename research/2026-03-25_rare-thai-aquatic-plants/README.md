## Why This Works

The hobbyist community is not buying plants. They are buying **trust that the plant arrives alive and pest-free**. Every Thai competitor fails at this. The 7-day survival guarantee, TC-only shipping, and proper phytosanitary documentation is the entire product — the plant is secondary.

---

## Why This Is Hard

1. Customs seizure risk — one snail = destroyed shipment
2. Western TC labs are slowly replicating Thai rarities in-house
3. New 19% US tariff (Aug 2025) increased landed cost by ~$7/plant
4. Direct-from-Thailand caps at ~$4k/month without a US transshipper
