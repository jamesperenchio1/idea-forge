# Logistics Guide — Thai Aquatic Plants Export
> Step-by-step with real costs | Updated 2026-03-25

---

## Overview

You are in Thailand. You buy plants. You ship them to buyers in the US, EU, or Japan. Here is exactly how that works, what it costs, and where things go wrong.

---

## The Two Formats You'll Ship

### Format A: Tissue Culture (TC) Cups ✅ Recommended
Sterile lab-grown plants in sealed agar/gel containers. No soil, no pests, no smell, 99% customs clearance rate.
- Best for: first shipments, US/EU buyers, rare/expensive species
- Shelf life in transit: 7–14 days safely
- Buy from: APC, Jeda Aquatics, Ratchaburi farms

### Format B: Bare Root
Plants grown emersed (above water), roots washed 100% soil-free, wrapped in damp paper towel, sealed in oxygen-filled poly bag.
- Best for: experienced buyers, bulk orders, species not available in TC
- Risk: "melt" (buyer puts in tank and plant dies back). Manage this with TC-only policy or clear warnings.

## Step-by-Step Export Process

### Step 1 — Source the Plants
**Where:**
- Chatuchak (JJ) Market, Bangkok — Plant Zone, **Wednesday & Thursday morning only**
- Facebook group: **ตลาดกลางพรรณไม้น้ำ** (Central Aquatic Plant Market) — post a "WTB" (want to buy) listing for wholesale lots
- Direct from Ratchaburi/Nakhon Pathom farms — drive there or hire a runner (฿300–500/day)

**Cost per plant (TC cups):**
| Species Tier | Thailand Buy Price |
|-------------|-------------------|
| Common (Anubias, basic Buces) | ฿50–150 ($1.50–$4.50) |
| Rare (Ludwigia White, Ghost Buces) | ฿200–400 ($6–$12) |
| Ultra-rare (wild Eriocaulon, Silver Queen) | ฿500–1,500 ($15–$45) |

---

### Step 2 — Get the Documents (Do This First, Not After)
Two documents are required. Get them in parallel before you pack anything.

**Document 1: Phytosanitary Certificate**
- Issuer: Thai Department of Agriculture (กรมวิชาการเกษตร)
- Purpose: Certifies plants are pest-free
- Official government fee: **฿100 ($3)**
- Agent service fee (handles inspection + paperwork): **฿1,500–2,000 ($45–$60)**
- Processing time: 1–3 business days
- Use a plant export agent for your first few shipments — worth every baht

**Document 2: Certificate of Unlisted Aquatic Species**
- Issuer: Thai Department of Fisheries (กรมประมง)
- Purpose: Confirms your plants are NOT on CITES protected list
- Cost: **฿200–500 ($6–$15)**
- Processing time: 1–2 business days
- Required for: all aquatic plants not appearing on CITES lists

**What you do NOT need for most aquarium plants:**
- CITES permit (only required for Crinum thaianum — which you should never sell)
- Export license (individuals can export ornamental plants without a trade license under a certain value)

**Total document cost per shipment: ฿1,800–2,600 (~$55–$75)**

---

### Step 3 — Pack the Shipment

**Materials (buy at Makro or JIB for packaging supplies):**

| Item | Cost |
|------|------|
| 4-mil polyethylene bags (50-pack) | ฿80 |
| Oxygen sachets (for bare root) | ฿5 each |
| Insulated shipping box (padded) | ฿150–250 |
| Bubble wrap / tissue paper | ฿50 |
| Ice pack (for hot season, Apr–Jun) | ฿30–60 |

**TC Cup packing:**
1. Wrap each cup in bubble wrap
2. Pack tightly in insulated box so cups don't shift
3. No water, no damp materials needed
4. Write "Aquatic Plants - TC Culture - No Soil" on outer box

**Total packing cost per 50-unit shipment: ฿400–600 (~$12–$18)**

---

### Step 4 — Choose Your Carrier

**DHL Express** ✅ Best option
- Transit time: 2–4 days to USA, 2–3 days to EU
- Cost (1kg box): **฿1,400–1,800 ($42–$54)**
- Cost (0.5kg box): **฿900–1,200 ($27–$36)**
- Drop-off: DHL service points across Bangkok, or schedule pickup
- Track: real-time, reliable, rarely lost
- Live animal/plant experience: DHL handles this regularly

**FedEx International Priority**
- Transit: 2–3 days to USA/EU
- Cost: similar to DHL, sometimes ฿200–400 cheaper on specific routes
- Good docs support if shipment gets held

**Thai Post EMS** ❌ Avoid for live plants
- Transit: 7–14 days
- Too slow — plants will arrive dead or stressed
- Fine for dried goods, seeds, not live TC cups

---

### Step 5 — Declare & Ship

At the DHL counter or when booking online:
- Customs description: **"Live Aquatic Plants - Tissue Culture - Ornamental"**
- HS Code: **0601.20.00** (live plants for ornamental purposes)
- Declared value: be honest — under-declaring causes issues if seized
- Attach both certificates to the outside of the box in a waterproof document pouch

---

### Step 6 — Buyer Clears Customs (USA Example)

Since Aug 2025:
- All shipments subject to **19% reciprocal tariff**
- Formal customs entry required for all commercial shipments
- Buyer pays: ~$6–9 per $35 plant in duties (pass this cost to buyer, price it in)
- US buyers need their own USDA PPQ 587 import permit — free, approved in 24 hours
- Buyer should apply for this before ordering

**For EU buyers:** generally easier — most EU countries accept TC aquatic plants without issue if phytosanitary cert is present.

**For Japan:** strictest inspections. Add ฿500–800 for additional documentation. Not recommended for beginners.

## The 7-Day Guarantee (Your Moat)

No Thai exporter currently offers this. Here's how to make it work financially:

- Offer: "If your plant doesn't arrive alive or dies within 7 days of arrival, full replacement or refund."
- Bake the risk into price: at 77% margin, you can absorb 1 DOA per batch and still profit
- Requirement: buyer must submit photo within 7 days
- This single policy will separate you from every competitor

### Step 2 — Payment Processing
| Option | Fee | Notes |
|--------|-----|-------|
| Stripe | 2.9% + $0.30/transaction | Best for international, works from Thailand via Stripe Atlas |
| Paddle | 5% + $0.50 | Handles VAT/tax globally, easier for solo founders |

**Thailand note:** You cannot create a standard Stripe account from Thailand. Options:
- Stripe Atlas ($500 one-time — registers a US Delaware LLC)
- Paddle or Lemon Squeezy (no US entity required)
- Wise Business account as intermediary

### Step 3 — Delivery
- SaaS: user logs in, it just works
- Digital download: Gumroad (no US entity needed) or Lemon Squeezy
- API product: RapidAPI marketplace for discovery

### Step 4 — Support
- Crisp.chat (free tier) for live chat
- Linear or Notion for bug tracking
- Discord server for community (doubles as support + marketing)

### Step 5 — Analytics
- Plausible (€9/mo) or PostHog (free tier) — privacy-friendly, no cookie banner needed
- Stripe Dashboard for revenue tracking
